Comprehensive Nutrient Guide

Macronutrients

  • Nitrogen (N): Essential for vegetative growth, protein synthesis, and chlorophyll formation.
  • Phosphorus (P): Crucial for root development, flowering, and energy transfer (ATP).
  • Potassium (K): Regulates water use, improves disease resistance, and aids in fruit formation.

Secondary Nutrients

  • Calcium (Ca): Strengthens cell walls and prevents disorders like blossom end rot.
  • Magnesium (Mg): The central atom in chlorophyll; essential for photosynthesis.
  • Sulfur (S): Required for the synthesis of certain amino acids and proteins.

Micronutrients

  • Zinc (Zn): Necessary for enzyme systems and growth hormone production.
  • Iron (Fe): Important for chlorophyll synthesis.
  • Boron (B): Critical for cell wall formation, cell division, and reproduction.
